# 🎯 CVC Learning App

An interactive, engaging app designed to help Kindergarten students master CVC (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant) words through fun, hands-on learning.

---

## 👩‍🏫 Created For
- Kindergarten students
- Early readers
- Classroom use & small group instruction

---

## ✨ Core Features
- 🔤 Build-the-word activities
- 🔊 Sound blending practice
- 🎮 Interactive tapping and dragging
- 🌈 Bright, kid-friendly design
- 🏆 Reward system (stars, sounds, celebrations)

---

## 🎮 Game Modes (Planned)
1. **Word Builder**
   - Students drag letters to form CVC words

2. **Tap the Sound**
   - Tap beginning, middle, or ending sound

3. **Read & Match**
   - Match words to pictures

---

## 🚀 Future Features
- Voice interaction (student says the word)
- Progress tracking for each student
- Teacher dashboard
- Custom word lists

---

## 💡 Purpose
This app was created to make phonics instruction more engaging, effective, and fun for young learners.
